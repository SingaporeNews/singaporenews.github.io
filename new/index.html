The Singapore Story

as told by the Straits Times

The Straits Times covers a vast trove of news stories over a period of 170 years, spanning many critical checkpoints in Singapore’s history. Distilling the contemporary mood of the era through the myriad stories is a daunting task, but technology may aid the process. Stories have been compiled in digital form via online archives of the National Library Board which enables deep data analysis methods on the news.

The following visualization looks at the key words that have been used in each year of the Straits Times and presents a dynamic portrayal of how words have evolved over time, suggesting an ebb and flow in tandem with the nation’s history.
